Yellow Sea

Description The OSMOSE-YS model simulates the life cycle of 13 focus species, from the egg stage to terminal age, with a time step of 3 months and a spatial resolution grid of 10 x 10 km². To represent the Yellow Sea food web, the model also includes two plankton groups (phytoplankton and zooplankton) as food sources, linking all species through trophic interactions. Currently, the model configuration is maintained but will be modified with model upgrades.
