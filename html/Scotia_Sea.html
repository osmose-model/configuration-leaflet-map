Cooperation Sea

Description: OSMOSE-CooperationSea model was developed in 2023 and simulated the ecosystem dynamic of Cooperation Sea, Southern Ocean. The model configuration is maintained by Jianye Tang (Shanghai Ocean University) and Lei Xing (Polar Research Institute of China).

Reference paper: L. Xing, J. Tang, S. Tian, and N. Barrier, « Simulating impacts of fishing toothfish on the pelagic community in the cooperation sea, southern ocean, » Regional studies in marine science, vol. 68, 2023. Download
