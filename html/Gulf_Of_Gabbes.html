Gulf of Gabes

Description: The Gulf of Gabes is a large continental shelf, exclusively composed of soft sediment resulting in the prevalence of bottom trawling activities. This application has been developed to investigate fishing impacts at different levels of biological organization and compare the performance of a set of ecosystem indicators in assessing these impacts.

Reference paper:G. Halouani, F. Lasram, Y. Shin, L. Velez, P. Verley, T. Hattab, R. Oliveros-Ramos, F. Diaz, F. Ménard, M. Baklouti, A. Guyennon, R. Ms, and F. Le Loc’h, “Modelling food web structure using an end-to-end approach in the coastal ecosystem of the gulf of gabes (tunisia)”, Ecological modelling, vol. 339, pp. 45-57, 2016. Download
