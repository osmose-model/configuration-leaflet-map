Jiaozhou Bay

Description: OSMOSE-JZB model was developed in 2017 and simulated the ecosystem dynamic of Jiaozhou Bay, China. The model configuration is not maintained by the Ren Lab (Ocean University of China).

Reference paper: Xing L, Zhang C, Chen Y, et al. An individual-based model for simulating the ecosystem dynamics of Jiaozhou Bay, China. Ecological Modelling, 2017, 360: 120-131. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ecolmodel.2017.06.010

Contact persons: Ren Lab (Ocean University of China)
