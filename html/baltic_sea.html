Baltic Sea

Description: The configuration simulates de fisheries in the western Baltic Sea: cod, sprat, plaice and herring for the period 1994 to 2019. It is currently under development and actively maintained. It takes plankton input from the ERGOM biogeochemical model and benthic biomass from a habitat model based on field observations.

Reference paper: Ongoing development
