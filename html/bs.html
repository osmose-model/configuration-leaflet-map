Black Sea

Description: The configuration simulates the fisheries in the Black Sea: anchovy, sprat, Mediterranean horse mackerel, red mullet, whiting, turbot, Atlantic bonito and bluefish for the period 2000 to 2019. It is currently under development and actively maintained. It takes zooplankton input from the GFDL-MOM6-COBALT2 physical/biogeochemical model.

Reference paper: Ongoing development; however, a master’s thesis is available here
